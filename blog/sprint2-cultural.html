Learning Plan

09/02/2021

My goal at Dev Academy is to prepare for a job as a web developer. Long-term I would like to find a flexible (preferably remote) job, so I can still have time for my kids. During my time here, I would love to feel like I belong in the tech community and understand how the day-to-day life of a developer is.

I am determined, quick to learn, curious and organised. I have to improve my ability to research and learn how to rely on others.

I plan to allocate time every day for my studies and communicate with my colleagues and facilitator often. I am committed to seeking help on Slack channels when I need it. I won't allow myself to be stuck for too long.

I expect the facilitation team to be honest in their feedback, non-judgemental and kind. And to be available when I need help.

Regarding family commitments, my husband and I made plans even before I started, so I don't think I'll have problems there.
